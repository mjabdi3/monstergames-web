How to Play?

When you collect and purchase a Monster, you will have access to Monster’s unique gaming platform, where you can compete with other collectors. Each Monster begins with 10 points, which you will be required to split them among 5 critical skills: Passion Ray, Brawn, Thrust, dexterity, and Rara Avis. You can take part in battles with other users in multiple game modes, from 1v1, 2v2 and 5v5 clashes. Monster Game’s Tournament events, Arcade Games, and exploring Monster Universe in the Metaverse.

Remember you can go around and use all your skills in each round of combat, you only get to use one skill per round.
